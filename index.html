Why carbon markets?

Renewable technologies

Invest directly in renewable projects that build a sustainable energy grid, generate carbon-neutral energy generation, and dismantle legacy policies that perpetuate the climate status quo.

Carbon Sequestration

Invest in the carbon-negative technologies that pull already-emitted carbon from the atmosphere, fixing it in construction materials, bio fuels, and mineralized carbon deposits deep underground.

Nature

Keep valuable, carbon-rich ecosystems, like the Amazon rainforest, South Asian mangroves, and the Louisiana Bayou out of the hands of developers with investments that maintain them in their natural state.
